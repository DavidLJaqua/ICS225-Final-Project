Ways to Help!

Purrfect Kitties is built off of community support!
We make this possible by helpful humans like you who donate time, money and supplies to our nonprofit organization!
Without your help, we would no longer be able to provide this awesome service to the public in the North Metropolitan Area.

If you would like to help supply us with the necessary food and shelters for our cats, we're always accepting donations to the cause!
To get in contact with us about donating supplies, time, or other resources, please feel free to contact us on our Contact Page.
